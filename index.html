Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a wireless researcher/research scientist in Wireless Network Research Department, led by Dr. Chen SUN, Research and Development Center (China Laboratory), SONY.

I received my bachelor degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013 and master degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2016, respectively. Thanks to my supervisors Prof. Laurent CLAVIER and Dr. Malcolm EGAN, I defended my Ph.D thesis "Impulsive and Dependent Interference in IoT Networks" on 8th December, 2020. During my Ph.D, I was also supervised by Prof. Jean-Marie GORCE and Prof. Gareth W. Peters. In 2019, I got a chance to visit Aalborg University, hosted by Prof. Troels PEDERSEN and Prof. Petar POPOVSKI.

My previous work includes impulsive and dependent interference (alpha-stable distribution and copula theory) and stochastic geometry.

My current research focuses on Federated Learning (FL), XR, Vehicle Mound Relay (VMR), etc.

I am also a delegate in 3GPP SA2 and an industrial supervisor in ZJU-UIUC
